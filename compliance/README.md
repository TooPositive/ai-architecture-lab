# Compliance

The EU AI Act enforcement begins August 2026. These guides help engineers understand what they need to build.

- [Overview](eu-ai-act/overview.md) — Act summary for engineers (coming soon)
- [Risk Classification](eu-ai-act/risk-classification.md) — How to classify your AI system (coming soon)

## Methodology

Compliance content is generated through manual analysis (not autonomous agents) to ensure accuracy. Legal text is mapped to specific technical requirements with implementation patterns.

This is not legal advice. Consult qualified counsel for your specific situation.
